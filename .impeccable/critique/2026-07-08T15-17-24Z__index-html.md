## Priority Issues

### P1: Mobile hash/deep-link behavior is fragile
The #work mobile screenshot opened on a large blank paper field before selected work. This undermines trust and suggests hash scrolling, hero layout settlement, or reveal timing needs tightening.

Fix: reproduce in browser, add explicit scroll-margin/scroll-padding as needed, and bound the mobile hero so the hash route lands predictably.

### P1: Proof metrics need provenance
600%, 6,000%, $200M+, and 10,000% are impressive but fragile without source context. They may make a hiring manager ask whether the claims can be defended.

Fix: add small source/provenance lines, or keep only the most defensible 2-3 metrics on the homepage and move heavier claims to project/system pages.

### P2: Marketing systems still feels like AI editorial scaffolding
The 01..06 sequence trips the detector and makes the section feel like a template.

Fix: replace numbers with concrete system types such as Dashboard, Content ops, Proposal engine, Publishing handoff, Prospecting map, Tool index.

### P2: Selected work is too normalized into equal cards
Real screenshots are strongest when they are art-directed. Four equal cards flatten the hierarchy and feel more generic.

Fix: make one lead case larger, use supporting cards around it, and avoid equalizing all proof artifacts.

### P2: Homepage becomes too systems-led after selected work
The page reads portfolio-first until selected work, then drifts into internal operating ledger density.

Fix: soften Marketing systems into a lighter bridge, keep heavier system detail on deeper pages, and make Results more compact/source-safe.

### P2: Mobile card title overflow risk
The digital out-of-home proposal card title appears clipped or cramped on mobile.

Fix: use text-wrap: balance, overflow-wrap where needed, and slightly smaller mobile card heading size.

### P2: Media/reduced-motion performance needs hardening
Hero video and poster assets are heavy, and reduced motion disables CSS animation but not autoplay video.

Fix: lazy-load below-fold images, add decoding=async, serve smaller/compressed hero assets where possible, and pause/swap video under prefers-reduced-motion.

## Persona Red Flags

Hiring manager: Believes the craft, but may question unsupported proof metrics.

Founder or marketing leader: Understands the general offer, but still has to translate AI visibility, queryable data, and operating layer into outcomes.

Design-sensitive reviewer: Sees competence, but may recognize the paper-grid/serif/mono/numbered-list lane before they see the proof.

## Recommended First Fixes

1. Fix mobile #work/deep-link behavior.
2. Add provenance or reduce homepage metrics.
3. De-number the systems list.
4. Re-art-direct selected work so one project leads.
5. Reduce systems/results density on the homepage.
