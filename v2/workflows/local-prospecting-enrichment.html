Selected workflow · Sales intelligence

Local prospecting and enrichment

Turns public local-business signals and nearby media inventory into reviewed, relevant opportunities for a sales team.

01 · Purpose

Why it exists

Local media sales depends on more than finding a business near a screen. The system makes location, category, public business context, and inventory proximity inspectable in one place, helping a seller start with a credible reason to reach out rather than a generic list.

02 · Sequence

How it works

Discovery and enrichment are automated where the signal is public and repeatable; opportunity fit and outreach remain reviewed.

  1. 01

    Discover businesses in Maps

    Search approved geographies and categories to assemble a location-aware set of potential local advertisers.

  2. 02

    Collect public business signals

    Capture available website, category, location, review, and business-status context without entering private systems.

  3. 03

    Enrich the working record

    Normalize domains, contact surfaces, category language, and other useful public fields while preserving source provenance.

  4. 04

    Match nearby screen inventory

    Compare business locations and intended reach with approved media inventory and meaningful proximity.

  5. 05

    Draft the opportunity note

    Summarize why the business, audience, moment, and nearby inventory may fit, including uncertainty or missing evidence.

  6. 06

    Hand off for sales review

    Route the record, sources, match rationale, and suggested angle to a human owner before any outreach.

03 · Sources

Tools and data sources

Only approved public sources and internal inventory are used in the public-safe workflow boundary.

  • Google Maps
  • public business websites
  • public business listings
  • geocoding
  • screen inventory
  • distance matching
  • working CRM records
  • human review queue

Output

What ships

A reviewed prospect record with source links, normalized business context, nearby inventory, match reasoning, and a suggested outreach angle.

Review

What stays human

Fit, sensitivity, account ownership, opportunity priority, final contact choice, message language, and the decision to reach out.

04 · Boundary

Public-safe proof and constraints

The workflow uses public business information and approved inventory, not private personal data or invented contact details. Distance does not equal fit, enrichment confidence is visible, and no business is contacted automatically. Public examples remove sensitive prospect records and internal commercial terms.
