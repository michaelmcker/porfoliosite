Agency management dashboard

Connects approved data sources, performance signals, content queues, and delivery reviews in one queryable operating surface.

01 · Purpose

Why it exists

An agency can have useful data in many systems and still lack a reliable operating view. This dashboard brings source status, client evidence, content work, AI-visibility signals, and delivery queues into a shared surface so teams can ask better questions without treating a static report as the source of truth.

02 · Sequence

How it works

The dashboard is an evidence and routing layer. It does not replace the systems that own the underlying data.

  1. 01

    Connect approved sources

    Register the client, permissions, reporting sources, content inventory, and delivery records that are allowed into the workspace.

  2. 02

    Check source health

    Surface stale connections, missing periods, broken fields, and other conditions that would weaken the evidence.

  3. 03

    Normalize a queryable surface

    Map unlike source structures into consistent client, channel, query, content, and delivery records.

  4. 04

    Surface movement and gaps

    Bring forward meaningful changes, AI-visibility gaps, Reddit or answer-engine signals, and work that needs an owner.

  5. 05

    Route review queues

    Turn signals into inspectable queues for research, content, reporting, or client follow-up without auto-publishing.

  6. 06

    Review delivery and reporting

    Use the shared evidence to prepare client-safe explanations, confirm completion, and decide the next operating action.

03 · Sources

Tools and data sources

The dashboard is connector-aware and permission-bound; sources vary by client and workstream.

  • Search Console
  • analytics and ad platforms
  • content inventory
  • AI visibility results
  • Reddit and web signals
  • Notion or Linear
  • client reporting records
  • delivery queues

Output

What ships

A source-health view, queryable client evidence, prioritized opportunity and delivery queues, and reviewed reporting inputs.

Review

What stays human

Access approval, source interpretation, client-safe explanations, priority decisions, work assignment, and every external publish or send.

04 · Boundary

Public-safe proof and constraints

Public examples use representative structures and approved aggregate outcomes; they omit client credentials, raw exports, private prompts, personal data, and unpublished work. A signal is not reported as a result until its source and comparison period are understood, and no dashboard queue silently becomes client-facing content.
